GREEN LANTERN

Green Lantern is a title held by several superheroes in the DC Comics universe, known for wielding power rings that grant them extraordinary abilities derived from willpower and imagination. The first Green Lantern, Alan Scott, discovered a mystical lantern after a train accident, which led him to create a ring from its material.

  • Hal Jordan, another prominent Green Lantern, is a test pilot whose life changed dramatically after witnessing his father's death in a plane crash, eventually becoming one of the most legendary Lanterns.

  • The Green Lanterns are part of a larger intergalactic police force, using their rings to fight evil and protect the universe.

THE LEKKI HEADMASTER

The Lekki Headmaster, written by Kabir Alabi Garba, is a novel that explores the challenges and triumphs of education in Nigeria. The story follows Mr. Adewale, a headmaster determined to turn around a failing school in Lekki, Lagos. The novel addresses real-life issues like corruption, resilience, teamwork, and hope. Mr. Adewale's efforts to improve the school's condition, despite numerous challenges, leave a lasting legacy of hope and excellence in the community. The novel is a must-read for those interested in African literature and the education system.
